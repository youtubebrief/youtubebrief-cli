# Code of Conduct

Youtubebrief is for developers, researchers, DevRel teams, and AI-agent workflow builders. We want the project to be useful, direct, and safe for contributors.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume good intent, but keep claims evidence-based.
- Share reproducible steps when reporting bugs.
- Protect private data: do not post API keys, private video links, customer data, cookies, tokens, or private transcripts.
- Keep criticism about the work, not the person.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, or sexualized attention.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, or private customer/video data.
- Repeated off-topic promotion or spam.
- Attempts to bypass rate limits, billing controls, security boundaries, or privacy protections.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove issues, pull requests, comments, and accounts from project spaces when needed to protect contributors or users.

Report conduct or safety concerns to `contact@youtubebrief.com`.
